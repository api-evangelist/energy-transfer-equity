# Energy Transfer Equity (energy-transfer-equity)

Energy Transfer Equity, L.P. (ETE) was a master limited partnership that owned and operated a diverse portfolio of midstream energy assets. In October 2018, ETE merged with its operating subsidiary Energy Transfer Partners to form a single publicly traded partnership, Energy Transfer LP (NYSE: ET). All developer resources, including the Messenger+ API for pipeline messaging and gas scheduling, are now provided under the Energy Transfer LP brand.
